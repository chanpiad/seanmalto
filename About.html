HISTORY

 
Sean Malto Wheels started as a wheel division of Powell Peralta, but has grown to stand as it's own brand behind a 
commitment to produce the highest quality urethane's available. Through continued improvements on present technologies, 
Sean Malto Wheels is available to offer a diverse line of specific formulations for all terrain types. The name of the 
company was taken from a skateboarder from Leavenworth, Kansas, United States (US). Sean Malto is of Caucasian and Filipino 
descent. He started skateboarding at the age of 10 at the hands of the inspiration of his older brothers Justin and Chris.
